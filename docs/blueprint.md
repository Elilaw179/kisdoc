# **App Name**: NutriGuide AI

## Core Features:

- Food Category Display: Showcase food categories with high-quality, appetizing images.
- Health Function Details: Explain the health benefits and nutritional information for each food category.
- AI Food Coach: AI-powered tool that guides users on food choices based on dietary needs and health goals, offering personalized recommendations.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Fresh avocado green (#A3CFA3) to evoke health and nature.
- Background color: Light, creamy off-white (#F7F4ED) for a clean, appetizing feel.
- Accent color: Muted gold (#D4AF37) for highlights and important information.
- Body text and headlines: 'Alegreya', a serif with an elegant, intellectual, contemporary feel that works well for longer blocks of text.
- Use clean, minimalist icons to represent food categories and dietary information.
- Employ a grid-based layout with plenty of white space to ensure a clean and easily navigable design.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user engagement, like smoothly expanding food categories.
